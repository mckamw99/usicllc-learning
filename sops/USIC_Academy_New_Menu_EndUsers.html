Academy Update · Effective June 22, 2026

USIC Academy has a fresh new look

As of Monday, June 22, 2026, USIC Academy switched to Docebo’s redesigned navigation menu. The update makes it easier to find your training and move around the platform. Your courses, your progress, and the way you log in did not change — only the look and layout of the menu.

★ What’s changed
 
 Same menu, same place. Your menu is still on the left side of the screen and still has all the items you use today — My Courses and Learning Plans, the Course Catalog, and more.
 
 Pin it open or tuck it away. A new Keep open / Auto-hide button at the top of the menu lets you keep it visible or let it slide out of the way to free up screen space.
 
 Your profile and Sign Out moved. Click your profile picture in the top-right corner to reach My Profile and Sign Out — they’re now in one tidy “My Account” spot instead of the side menu.
 
 A quick tour is waiting. The first time you sign in to the new menu, you can take a short guided tour to see what moved — or skip it and explore on your own.
 
✓ What you need to do
 

Nothing. The new menu turned on automatically on June 22. Just log in to USIC Academy the same way you always do (usic.training) and the updated look is ready and waiting for you.
