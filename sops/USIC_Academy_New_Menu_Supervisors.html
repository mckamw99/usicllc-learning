Supervisor Update · Effective June 22, 2026

A new USIC Academy menu — and a clearer view of your team

On Monday, June 22, 2026, USIC Academy is moving to Docebo’s redesigned navigation menu. The same change is rolling out to everyone, so your teams will see a refreshed layout too — you may get questions from them, so a quick heads-up is below. The bigger news for you: the new menu makes the My Team area easier to reach, giving you a real-time picture of your direct reports’ training.

★ The menu change at a glance
 
 The menu stays on the left side with all the same items. It can be pinned open or set to auto-hide using the button at the top.
 
 My Profile and Sign Out moved to the profile picture in the top-right corner (the new “My Account” area).
 
 No action needed — the change applies automatically. Logins (SSO) and all training records are unaffected.
 
★ Spotlight: Your My Team Dashboard

Your team is linked to you automatically — there’s nothing to set up. Just open the main menu (top-left) and click My Team to see everything your team is working on in one place:

 
 Team Members — a roster of everyone who reports to you, with a snapshot of where each person stands.
 
 Courses & Learning Plans — track your team’s progress and completions across every course and assigned learning plan.
 
 Spot what’s overdue — filter to show only training that is overdue, or due soon (within 14 days), so nothing slips through before a deadline.
 
 Reports — download reports on courses, learning plans, certifications, and ILT sessions for your team members.
 
 Drill into an individual — open any team member’s summary from the … menu on their tile to review their full training record.
