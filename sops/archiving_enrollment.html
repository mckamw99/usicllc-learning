Archiving Enrollment for Learning Plans and Courses

How Superadmins and Power Users archive course and learning plan enrollments in Docebo — clearing records from active view while preserving completion history.

Overview

01

Archiving an enrollment removes it from a learner’s active course and learning plan lists while keeping the underlying training record. It is the recommended way to clean up enrollment data — after a role change, a re-org, or when retiring outdated assignments — without losing the historical completion data needed for compliance and reporting. This guide covers archiving both course enrollments and learning plan enrollments, how to view archived records, and the options available at the point of archiving.

Archived is not deleted. Archiving hides an enrollment from active views but retains the learner’s completion and tracking history, so prior completions still surface in reports.

Before You Begin

02
Archived enrollments cannot be restored. Docebo stores archived enrollments in a different data structure from active ones, so the action is effectively permanent. If you may need to reactivate a learner later, unenroll them instead of archiving.
Completion data is preserved. Archiving keeps the historical training record intact — completions a learner already earned remain available for reporting after the enrollment is archived.
Archiving triggers notifications. The action fires any course, learning plan, and enrollment notifications you have configured. Before archiving in bulk, temporarily disable the relevant notifications to avoid flooding learners and managers.

Archiving a Course Enrollment

03
Vendor Resource: See the Docebo Help Article for additional reference.
  1. 1
    Navigate to the Courses PageIn the left sidebar, hover over the folder icon and select Content & Delivery › Courses.
  2. 2
    Open the CourseClick the title of the course to open its configuration page.
  3. 3
    Open Manage EnrollmentsIn the top right corner, click the Manage enrollments button to open the enrollments table.
  4. 4
    Locate the LearnerFind the user whose enrollment you want to archive. Use the Enrollment type filter to confirm you are viewing Active enrollments.
  5. 5
    Open the Row MenuClick the ellipsis menu (…) at the end of the user’s row and select Archive enrollment.
  6. 6
    Choose Options and ConfirmIn the pop-up, choose Archive and re-enroll or Archive and unenroll, optionally check Reset the tracking of the training materials, then click Archive enrollment. See the Options Reference for what each choice does.
Archiving several at once: Select the learners using the checkboxes at the start of each row, click Choose action at the bottom right of the table, and select Archive enrollment. The same options pop-up applies to the whole selection.

Archiving a Learning Plan Enrollment

04
Vendor Resource: See the Docebo Help Article for additional reference.
  1. 1
    Navigate to Learning PlansFrom the Admin Menu, select Learning Plans to open the learning plans list.
  2. 2
    Open the Learning PlanClick the title of the learning plan whose enrollments you want to manage.
  3. 3
    Go to the Enrollments TabMove to the Enrollments tab to list all users enrolled in the plan.
  4. 4
    Open the Row MenuClick the ellipsis menu (…) at the end of the user’s row and select Archive enrollment.
  5. 5
    Choose Options and ConfirmChoose Archive and re-enroll or Archive and unenroll, optionally check Reset the tracking of the training materials in the courses included in the learning plan, then confirm.
Archiving several at once: Select the learners using the checkboxes, click Choose action at the bottom of the table, and select Archive enrollment.
Archiving a plan enrollment cascades to its courses. When you archive a learning plan enrollment, the enrollments for every course in that plan are archived as well. However, if you choose Archive and unenroll and a course in the plan also belongs to another learning plan, the platform will not archive or unenroll that shared course from the other plans — shared courses are protected.
The plan itself is untouched. Archiving a learning plan enrollment does not archive the learning plan, its courses, or their training materials — only the selected user’s enrollment.

Viewing Archived Enrollments

05

Archived enrollments are hidden from the default (active) view but remain fully accessible for reference and reporting.

  1. 1
    Open the Enrollments PageFor a course, open Manage enrollments; for a learning plan, open the Enrollments tab.
  2. 2
    Open the Enrollment Type FilterClick Enrollment type above the enrollments table.
  3. 3
    Select Archived EnrollmentsChoose Archived enrollments. The table updates to show archived records. Switch back to Active enrollments to return to the default view.

Options Reference

06

The same three choices appear in the archive pop-up for both courses and learning plans:

OptionWhat it doesUse it when
Archive & re-enrollArchives the current record and immediately re-enrolls the learner in the same course or plan. History is kept and the learner keeps access.You want to reset or refresh an assignment while preserving the prior record.
Archive & unenrollArchives the record and removes the learner’s access. They can be re-enrolled manually later, or self-enroll if the enrollment policy allows.The learner should no longer have the course or plan.
Reset tracking checkboxAlso clears the learner’s progress so they restart the material. Left unchecked, a re-enrolled learner resumes and only takes material they had not yet completed (useful for pushing newly added content).You want the learner to retake content from the beginning.
